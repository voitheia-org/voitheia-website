## 📖 About Us

Millions of children around the world grow up with little to no awareness of community service, leaving countless opportunities for meaningful change unrealized. 

**Voitheia** began as an idea to allow students to engage with less fortunate children and learn from these invaluable experiences. Based in **Lagos, Nigeria**, we foster a sense of community to shape the mindset of young adults who will lead the future with the goal of helping each other.

## 🏗️ Our Work & Projects

We work closely with local orphanages and homes in Nigeria to bring resources, joy, and meaningful interaction to children. 

* **Heritage Homes:** Our pilot project. We gathered books, stationery, toys, and sweets, engaging with children through interactive games.
* **Little Saint's Girls Orphanage:** We donated essential supplies and engaged in heartfelt conversations, fostering deep empathy and understanding.
* **Bab Es Salaam Orphanage:** A larger-scale initiative where we organized sports and various games, providing prizes and an abundance of resources.

## 💻 Tech Stack & UI Features

This repository houses the source code for the Voitheia website. Our design language focuses on warmth, smooth interactivity, and accessibility.

**Frontend Core:**
* **HTML5 / CSS3:** Custom-built UI without heavy frameworks.
* **JavaScript:** Handling DOM manipulation, modals, and clipboard utilities.

**Design Highlights:**
* **Color Palette:** Warm Peach Background (`#FFF5EE`) paired with a vibrant Primary Coral (`#FF6B6B`).
* **Custom Interactivity:** A tailored mouse follower (`.cursor-follower`) to enhance the user experience.
* **Smooth Animations:** Custom `cubic-bezier` transitions, particle backgrounds (`particles.js`), and modern modal galleries.
