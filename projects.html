Heritage Homes

45 Faramobi Ajike St, Anthony, Lagos 105102, Lagos, Nigeria

Heritage Homes is our flagship initiative aimed at providing shelter, safety, and education to orphaned and vulnerable children. Located in Lagos, Nigeria, it serves as a nurturing environment where children grow with dignity, care, and opportunity.

We provide essentials like food, clothing, and medical care, along with access to formal education and personal mentorship. The home also offers emotional and psychological support, ensuring holistic development.
